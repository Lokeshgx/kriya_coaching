Who are We?

Kriya Coaching Classes

Kriya Coaching Classes is a premier educational institution committed to providing personalized, supplementary education that bridges the gaps in school learning. With over 15 years of experience, we have helped countless students achieve academic excellence and secure their dreams.

Our approach is student-centric, focusing on individual needs, learning styles, and pace. We believe that every student has the potential to succeed, and our role is to unlock that potential through dedicated mentorship and quality education.

We are not just an institute; we are a family of educators, parents, and students working together towards a common goal - academic excellence and overall development.

Our Mission

To empower students with comprehensive knowledge, problem-solving skills, and confidence to excel in academics and competitive examinations through personalized learning approaches and expert mentorship.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred coaching institute that transforms students' academic careers and creates confident, well-rounded individuals prepared for life's challenges and opportunities.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Inclusivity, Innovation, and Student-centricity. We maintain the highest standards while ensuring affordable quality education for all deserving students.
